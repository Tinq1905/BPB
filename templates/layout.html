Instructions

Welcome!

Business Plan Builder is an effective tool for start-up business. This platform would allow you to input information on all aspects of your business, and generate a well-organized business report. No matter you are seeking for financial support or trying to make a precise budget and forecast before the business take off.

The report are seperated into different parts. Each part would accepts information like numbers, words and mainly paragraphs. You are expected to go in every areas and leave some meanningful contents. The final report will use the exact information you typed in.

Info:Info page collects basic information about your business and company. All areas are required and submit button can be used to update information when required.

Executive summary:Executive summary page collects sections as high level description of the overall business. For each section there will be section tittles and contents. Feel free to edit your current sections and delete them by function buttons.

Suggested titles:
  • Overview
  • About the industry
  • Key successful factors
  • Key Chanllenges

    • SWOT Analysis:SWOT analysis is used to analyse Strength, Weakness, Opportunity, and Threats for the business. You can freely input the identified SWOTs and add them in.

    Customer Analysis:Customer analysis page collects sections about the potential customers of the business and related analysis. Feel free to edit your current sections and delete them by function buttons.

    Suggested titles:
  • Customer segmentation
  • Customer profile
  • Key requirements

    • Competitor Analysis:Competitor analysis page collects sections about the current competitors in the market and potential competitors. This includes comparision and strategy. Feel free to edit your current sections and delete them by function buttons.

    Suggested titles:
  • Big players in the market
  • Adventages and disadventages
  • Competition strategy

    • Industry Analysis:Industry analysis page collects sections about the currently situation, risks and challenges in the industry. Feel free to edit your current sections and delete them by function buttons.

    Suggested titles:
  • Industry outlook
  • Demand drivers
  • Major market

    • Product and Service:In this page, detailed information about the product and services are required. Feel free to edit your current sections and delete them by function buttons.

    Budget:Budget section contains four different tabs. The Revenue page allows you to input as many as items you want. Expense tab is used to input possible expense including labour cost, rents, bank fees, etc. Preview page gives a view of the calculation results of the stored data. Historical data is optional and it is only required when you already have running data for last two years.

    Risk:Risk page collects potential risks include their name, likelihood, impact, rating, and mitigation strategy. A table contains the risks will be generated based on the information in the final report.
